	Overview:

		Serpentine is a game I made for my final project in my Java class, Freshman year in
		college. It is based off of the original game simply called Snake, or in some places
		known as Nibbles. The game is simple enough; you play as a snake trying to eat as 
		many apples as you can. If you ever munch on your own tail, (or any other part of
		your snakey body,) you die. 


///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

	Controls:

		This game was designed not to need any documentation to play, and as such the game
		is controlled in a very simple manner. The only controls you have are the keyboard
		keys and two buttons on the right side of the screen. You can use your arrow keys 
		to guide your snake around the board, he will keep travelling in the direction you
		last pressed until a new direction is pressed. The Reset button will reset your 
		score and position, and allow you to play a new game once you have died. The exit
		button quits the game.


///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

	Features:

		- Fast-Paced Action
		- Randomly Generated Apples
		- Infinitely scrolling world
		- Flashing death animation!
		- Session-based High Scores
